What is Hyperspektiv?

Hyperspektiv is an app that allows you to experience and capture photos and videos of distorted reality.

How do I change the settings of a filter? How do I add mirrors?

Tap the selected filter to open it’s settings. Then, tapping the name of an effect allows you to make adjustments.

Below the effects settings you can add mirrors simply by tapping one on. Tap again to flip the axis. Tap once more to turn it off. You can combine multiple mirrors.

Can I adjust the effects live while recording?

Yes. Each filter has a predefined live effect. Simply drag your finger around the viewscreen to change the effects in realtime.

How do I use VJ Mode?

AirPlay

1. Connect your iPhone to an AirPlay enabled device, such as an Apple TV.
2. Turn mirroring on.
3. Go wild.

HDMI

1. Get a lightning to HDMI cable.
2. Connect an HDMI cable to a TV, projector, video mixer, VJ rig, etc.
3. Distort reality in real-time.

Try pointing Hyperspektiv at the display of choice to create feedback. It’s a lot of fun.

Can I adjust the effects live while recording?

Yes. Each filter has a predefined live effect. Simply drag your finger around the viewscreen to change the effects in realtime. Drag your finger down to the bottom left to return the filter to default state.

If you have 3D touch, push down on the view screen for an added dimension.

Which iOS devices do you support?

Currently we support iPhone 5s, iPhone 6, iPhone 6 Plus, iPhone 6s, iPhone 6s Plus running iOS 9.

How do I take a photo instead of video?

Tap the photo capture button to the left of the record button. The photo will be automatically saved to your camera roll.

What resolution can I capture in?

Hyperspektiv currently records in HD 720p resolution. We have plans to introduce the ability to record in 1080p very soon.

Does it only capture in square?

Nope! Tilt your device 90° into landscape mode to experience and capture video and photos in full widescreen.

How do I use the Wildcard filter?

Wildcard is a fun and experimental feature which randomizes all our effects and gives you truly unique and near-limitless possibilities. Simply tap the Wildcard filter, and each time you tap it you will get a new set of random effect combinations. Have fun with it.

I can’t see very much with some filters, what gives?

Some of our filters are sensitive to the amount of light coming in through the camera. Try moving into brighter or dimmer light for varied results.

Some of my videos look compressed or not as good on Instagram — why?

Currently Instagram adds compression when you upload videos. Unfortunately this means you may occasionally notice some compression artifacts or degradation in the finer details of your Hyperspektiv videos after they’re posted on Instagram. This is an issue with Instagram’s app and it’s something we hope they address some day.

Vine, Twitter and Facebook currently have settings which allow you to enable HD uploads which we strongly recommend when uploading videos made with Hyperspektiv.

How do I add music to my videos?

Currently we use a number of different apps to add music to our videos such as iMovie and Vine. Hyperspektiv videos really come to life with music.
